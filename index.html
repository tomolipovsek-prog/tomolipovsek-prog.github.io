📜 Marketplace Rules

Every product must clearly show botanical name, local names, origin, quantity, harvest year, storage notes and available certificates.

Seller is responsible for accurate product information, legal sourcing, safe packaging and timely shipment with tracking.

🛡️ Buyer Protection

Buyer funds remain protected until delivery is confirmed or the protection period expires without dispute.

🚚 Shipping Rules

All shipments should include tracking. Delivery country restrictions, customs rules and phytosanitary requirements must be respected.

Products that are illegal, invasive, unsafe, uncertified where required, or restricted by destination country may not be listed.
